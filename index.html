Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary language of instruction?

All courses are taught in a mix of English and Hindi ("Hinglish") for conceptual clarity. All study materials, however, are provided in English to match the exam standards.

Is the AAGAZ Batch sufficient for both CUET PG and UG?

Yes. The AAGAZ batch curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation that covers all topics required for both the CUET PG and UG History exams.

How are doubts resolved?

We have dedicated live doubt-clearing sessions with Amit Mehra himself, in addition to a 24/7 discussion forum, ensuring no student is left behind.
